Welcome to Pets Therapy!

Hi there! These terms explain how to enjoy Pets Therapy responsibly. We're the team at EosMate s.r.o, and we've created this app to bring a little joy and relaxation to your day.

By using our app, you're agreeing to these simple guidelines. Don't worry - we've made them as straightforward as possible!

Important: All the adorable digital pets, animations, and artwork in Pets Therapy belong to us (EosMate s.r.o). We've put a lot of love into creating them! Please enjoy them within the app, but don't share or use them elsewhere without asking first.

Our Creative Work

  • What's ours: All the cute pets, animations, sounds, and graphics you see in the app
  • Legal protection: Our artwork is protected by copyright (like all creative work!)
  • Pet designs: The unique look and personality of each pet is our trademark
  • Your experience: You get to enjoy and interact with our pets, but they remain our creative property

What We Ask You Not To Do

To keep things fair for everyone, please don't:

  • Extract or copy the pet graphics from the app
  • Share our pet artwork on other platforms (screenshots of the app are totally fine though!)
  • Use our pets in other projects or apps
  • Make your own versions of our specific pet designs
  • Sell or make money from our pet artwork
  • Try to peek behind the scenes at our code
  • Use our pets for business purposes (just ask us first - we're friendly!)

What You Can Totally Do

Feel free to:

  • Enjoy all the pets and have fun with the app!
  • Take screenshots for your own memories
  • Share screenshots on social media (we love when you mention us!)
  • Create your own custom pets using our built-in tools

Your Right to Use the App

What You Get

We're giving you permission to use Pets Therapy for your personal enjoyment! This is just for you - not for sharing or commercial use.

A Few Limits

  • You can't transfer this permission to someone else
  • The app content still belongs to us
  • If you break these rules, we might need to revoke access
  • We can update these permissions as needed

Being a Good Community Member

We're all here to relax and have fun! Please help us by:

  • Using the app for good, positive purposes
  • Respecting our creative work
  • Not trying to break or hack the app
  • Helping us maintain a friendly, welcoming environment

Things to Avoid

  • Trying to access parts of the app you shouldn't
  • Doing anything that might break the app for others
  • Uploading inappropriate content when creating custom pets
  • Pretending to be part of our team
  • Breaking any laws (this should go without saying!)

  • We can display your custom pets in the app (that's how they work!)

  • Make sure your creations follow these guidelines

Keeping Things Friendly

Please don't include:
